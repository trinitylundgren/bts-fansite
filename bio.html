Kim Namjoon / RM / 김남준

RM was born on September 12th, 1994 in Dongjak-gu, South Korea. He was the first member of BTS and is the leader and main rapper of the group, as well as a songwriter and producer.

  • He is the tallest member of BTS at 181 cm (5'11").
  • RM is nicknamed "The God of Destruction" for often breaking things around him.
  • He loves sweet snacks like cotton candy, chocolates and cakes.
  • His favorite colors are black, purple and pink.
  • He has composed or produced more than 100 songs.
  • He attended Global Cyber University and majored with honours in Broadcasting and Performing Art. He was among the top rankers in the national school exam and had a TOEIC score of over 900, which is commendable.
  • According to him, rapping helped him get over the stress of his studies and the lyrics of his song ‘No More Dreams’ was inspired by the days when he worked late into the night for his exams.
  • He is a vocal supporter of LGBT rights and describes himself as an atheist.

Kim Seokjin / Jin / 김석진

Jin was born on December 4th, 1992 in Gwacheon, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. Jin made his debut as one of the then four vocalists of BTS on June 13th, 2013.

  • He is "known for having an incredibly handsome face" and was scouted by BTS's label Big Hit Entertainment for his looks while walking down the street.
  • At 179 centimeters (5'10"), he is the second-tallest member of the band.
  • He is a tenor and can play the guitar.
  • He is the eldest member of BTS.
  • His favorite color is pink. His second album even shows him with pink hair.
  • Fans say that of all the bandmates, he is the best at expressing his emotions through song.
  • In December 2018, Jin donated various supplies to the Korean Animal Welfare Association to celebrate his birthday, purchasing food, blankets, and dishes for the organization.
  • He is well known for his athletic gifts, which include proficiency in snowboarding, tennis and golf. During a local TV show, he even managed to do well at wakeboarding on his first try.
  • He currently has two sugar gliders, Eomuk and Odeng. Both names come from Korean words for fish cakes.

Min Yoongi / Suga / 민윤기

Suga was born on March 9th, 1993 in Daegu, South Korea. Suga is a rapper, songwriter and record producer who made his debut with BTS in 2013.

  • Prior to joining Big Hit Entertainment, Suga used to be an underground rapper with the stage name Gloss.
  • Due to his straightforward and realistic personality, some of his mixtape songs contain hard-hitting words and critical lines.
  • Suga loves basketball. He used to play basketball a lot as a student, and when he was a trainee, he played it every Sunday. His position was usually point guard or shooting guard.
  • Suga loves taking photos. He currently majors in photography at Global Cyber University.
  • Suga has a habit of biting his nails.
  • Suga likened himself to a cat because he likes to be alone and doesn’t mingle easily with others.
  • Suga’s favorite color is white.
  • The first album he purchased was one by Eminem.
  • He doesn’t like dancing despite being good at it. Other dislikes include loud and crowded places.

Jung Hoseok / J-Hope / 정호석

J-Hope was born on February 18th, 1994 in Gwangju, South Korea. J-Hope is a rapper, songwriter, dancer and record producer who made his debut with BTS in 2013.

  • J-Hope dreamed of becoming a professional tennis player during his elementary school years.
  • He is 177 centimeters tall (5'9") and weighs 65 kilograms (143 lbs).
  • His favorite color is green.
  • J-Hope had tough times during his trainee days, prompting him to leave the team for a while. His bandmates persuaded him to come back, and then they made their debut as BTS.
  • J-Hope loves traditional Korean foods such as kimchi.
  • His favorite BTS choreography includes that for “Save Me” and “DNA.”
  • J-Hope is the only BTS member who doesn’t have any piercings. He is scared of needles.
  • J-Hope’s Myers-Briggs personality type is ESFJ: extroverted, sensing, feeling, judging.
  • For J-Hope, the three elements of happiness are family, health and love.
  • His motto in life is, “If you don’t work hard, there won’t be good results.”

Park Jimin / Jimin / 박지민

Jimin was born on October 13th, 1995 in in Geumjeong District, Busan, South Korea. Jimin is a singer, songwriter, and dancer who made his debut with BTS in 2013.

  • He attended the Busan High School of Arts, where he specialized in modern dance. A dance teacher at the high school suggested that he audition to become a K-pop star. Taking the advice, he was later accepted by Big Hit Entertainment as a trainee.
  • When he first moved to Seoul, Jimin said he was self-conscious of his Busan accent.
  • He was the final member to join BTS, with the shortest training period.
  • Jimin is one of the hardest working members, according to his bandmates. He practices constantly every day when he has spare time.
  • Three things he likes: Jungkook, performing and getting attention.
  • He is 173 centimeters tall (5'8") and weighs 60 kilograms (132 lbs).
  • Applying eyeliner is crucial for him; without eyeliner, he said he is unable to dance hip-hop or have strong expressions because he gets really shy.
  • Jimin’s favorite food is meat, fruits and any type of stew. He really likes kimchi as well.
  • His fans affectionately call him “Jungkook’s Mom,” as he openly cares a lot about Jungkook, the youngest member of the group.
  • “Dragon Ball” was one of his favorite comics growing up. He enjoys going to comic book cafes in his free time.

Kim Taehyung / V / 김태형

V was born on December 30th, 1995 in in Seo District, Daegu, South Korea. Jimin is a singer, songwriter, record producer and actor who made his debut with BTS in 2013.

  • V's stage name means 'victory.'
  • His cute habits include biting his nails, leaving his mouth open, and saying "apa!" (it hurts) and "eomma" (mommy).
  • His favorite colors are black, white, and green.
  • He is yet to be in a serious relationship because he wants to marry his first love and be with her forever.
  • He is a dancer and a vocalist, known for his deep, husky voice.
  • He played the saxophone for three years in middle school. He thought about becoming a saxophonist, but he decided he liked singing more.
  • V’s role model is his father, whom he describes as a good listener who takes good care of his children. The singer’s goal in life is to be just like his dad.
  • He is 178 centimeters tall (5'10") and weighs 63 kilograms (139 lbs).
  • V doesn’t like coffee because of the bitter taste. But he likes drinking hot chocolate.
  • V said his long, thin hands were the part of his body he’s most confident about.
  • V doesn’t drink alcohol. He drinks Coke instead of beer.
  • V has a dog named Soonshim, who lives with his parents. His new puppy, a black teacup Pomeranian named Yeontan, lives with him now.
  • His bandmates say he has the most outgoing personality in the group.

Jeon Jungkook / Jungkook / 전정국

Jimin was born on September 1st, 1997 in in Busan, South Korea. Jungkook is a singer, songwriter, and record producer who made his debut with BTS in 2013.

  • He once dreamed of becoming a badminton player.
  • He joined the group at 15, becoming its youngest member.
  • His favorite foods include cheese, melons and apple mango.
  • During his school days, he disliked all academic subjects but liked physical education, art and music.
  • His hobbies are drawing, playing guitar and watching movies.
  • He is 178 centimeters tall (5'10") and weighs 61 kilograms (134 lbs).
  • He was a big fan of the anime “Shugo Chara!” in his childhood. He still likes watching animated movies such as “Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day,” “The Girl Who Leapt Through Time,” “Spirited Away” and “5 Centimeters per Second.”
  • Red is his favorite color. That’s why he is frequently spotted wearing red.
  • Jungkook’s nickname is “Golden Youngest,” and his mother dreamed about gold before he was born (part of a Korean tradition).
  • He used to like playing the shooting game Overwatch with V.
  • His motto in life is, “It would be better to die than to live without passion.”
